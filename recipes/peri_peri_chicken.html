Peri Peri Chicken Recipe

Description

If you love Nandos, you'll love this homemade recipe! This is a super easy recipe that anybody can do and it can be cooked up in no time at all.

Ingredients:

  • 125g bottle of Peri-Peri sauce
  • 12 boneless chicken thighs with skin on

Steps:

  1. Put the chicken thighs in a bowl and rub all over with the sauce.
  2. Cover the bowl with cling film and put in the fridge for at least an hour.
  3. Heat the oven to 180c (standard not fan) or gas mark 4.
  4. Transfer the chicken thighs to a roasting tray skin side up.
  5. cook for approx 20 minutes or until the chicken is just cooked.
  6. Heat up a grill pan and put the chicken on skin side down.
  7. Leave the thighs without moving them to create the grill marks.
  8. Turn the thighs over once the skin has the grill marks.
  9. Brush over with your chosen Nando's sauce.
